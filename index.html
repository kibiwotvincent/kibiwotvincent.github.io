Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get a moving quote?

Simply fill out our online form or call us directly. We'll provide a free, no-obligation quote tailored to your specific needs.

How early should I book my move with GS-MOVERS?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance to secure your preferred date and time, especially during peak seasons.

Do you offer packing and unpacking services?

Yes! We offer professional packing, unpacking, and even packing materials to make your move completely stress-free.

Is my furniture and belongings insured during the move?

Absolutely. We handle your items with care, and our service includes basic insurance coverage. Additional coverage options are also available.

Can you help move heavy or fragile items like pianos and antiques?

Definitely. Our team specializes in handling heavy, delicate, and valuable items with the right equipment and techniques.

How long will my move take?

Every move is different! The time depends on the distance, amount of items, and access to your properties. We'll provide an accurate estimate after a quick survey.

What should I do to prepare before the movers arrive?

We recommend labeling boxes, securing valuables separately, and reserving parking if needed. We'll also guide you through a full checklist after booking!

What if my moving date changes?

No worries - just contact us as soon as possible. We're flexible and will do our best to adjust to your new schedule.

Do you disassemble and reassemble furniture?

Yes, we can disassemble and reassemble beds, desks, and other large furniture pieces as part of your moving package.
